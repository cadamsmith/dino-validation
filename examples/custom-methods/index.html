Custom Method: Age Verification

This example demonstrates a custom ageVerification method that:

  • Validates the date of birth is a valid date
  • Ensures the date is not in the future
  • Calculates age from the birth date
  • Verifies the user is at least 18 years old
  • Ensures the age is within a reasonable range (max 120 years)

This type of complex validation logic is perfect for custom methods.
